The Atari was one of the first home video game systems ever to be made, being released in 1977. The original Atari only had the game Pong, which was a game where you hit a ball back and forth across the screen attempting to get it past your opponent, and the Atari was controlled using a joystick.

In 1982, a game for the atari released called Yars' Revenge, and it was available on the third generation of the Atari Video Computer System.
