The Nintendo Gamecube is a sixth generation home gaming console. It was released in 2001, about 5 years after the N64. The Gamecube controller is renowned for its ingenious design by people everywhere, and it is still considered one of the best controllers to this day. The Gamecube ran games off of small optical discs, making it the first Nintendo console to do so.
Here is a list of some games for the Gamecube:

  • Super Smash Bros. Melee
  • Mario Kart: Double Dash!!
  • Super Mario Sunshine
  • Luigi's Mansion
  • The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker
